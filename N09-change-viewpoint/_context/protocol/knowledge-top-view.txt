========================================
GOAL
========================================

Generate a precise "Orthographic TOP View" of the architecture presented in the source image.
Treat the building in the image as a completed, constructed reality.
Change the angle of view to this specific new perspective without altering the building's original geometry, materials, or style.
Render the simulation strictly by resetting the Angle of View and the Optical Environment.

========================================
PHOTOGRAPHER'S WORKFLOW
========================================

Manual Entry: Capturing the Orthographic Top View
This professional technique creates a perfectly flat, non-perspective, two-dimensional image of a building's roof — a plan view.

POSITIONING:
  Place the camera (drone) at sufficient altitude, positioned directly and vertically above the center of the building.
  Camera must point straight down at exactly 90 degrees (nadir position).

PHOTOGRAPHIC FOCUS:
  Capture the graphic composition of the roof plane.
  Focus on the building's overall footprint, the geometric relationship between forms and voids (courtyards, terraces), and the patterns created by roofing materials and structures.
  Produce a clear, diagram-like representation of the building from above.

LIGHTING:
  Shoot under the soft, even, diffused light of an overcast day.
  This eliminates all shadows, allowing the pure geometry and texture of the roof plan to be documented with maximum clarity.

TECHNIQUE AND PERSPECTIVE CONTROL:
  This view requires a true orthographic projection.
  All sense of perspective must be eliminated, resulting in a perfect two-dimensional image.
  Frame the composition in a 1:1 square aspect ratio to reinforce the plan-like quality.

SPECIFICATION

APERTURE: f/8
ISO: 100
SHUTTER_SPEED: 1/250 sec
OTHER_EQUIPMENT: Vertical Descent Shooting Mode (securing accurate vertical view)

========================================
IMAGE GENERATION PARAMETERS
========================================

PROMPT_RULES:
  - Simulate a perfectly vertical top-down orthographic view (nadir, 90 degrees straight down)
  - Eliminate all perspective — render as true orthographic projection
  - Show roof plane and footprint clearly with no vanishing points
  - Preserve all original geometry, materials, and proportions — no alteration
  - Apply soft overcast lighting (no directional shadows, maximum roof clarity)
  - Aspect ratio: 1:1 square

STYLE_PARAMS:
  rendering_style: orthographic top-view architectural plan photography
  camera_height: directly above, nadir position
  camera_angle: 90 degrees straight down
  perspective: orthographic, no vanishing points
  lighting: overcast diffused, shadowless, even illumination

NEGATIVE_PROMPT:
  cartoon, illustration, sketch, watermark, blur,
  perspective view, vanishing points, angled view, tilted view,
  altered geometry, added elements, directional shadows, ground-level elements

QUALITY_GATE:
  If image shows perspective distortion or vanishing points → regenerate once
  If building footprint is not visible from directly above → regenerate once
  If aspect ratio is not 1:1 → note in generation-spec
