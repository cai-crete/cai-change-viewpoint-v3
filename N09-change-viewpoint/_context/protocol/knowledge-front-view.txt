========================================
GOAL
========================================

Generate a precise "Front Elevation View" of the architecture presented in the source image.
Treat the building in the image as a completed, constructed reality.
Change the angle of view to this specific new perspective without altering the building's original geometry, materials, or style.
Render the simulation strictly by resetting the Angle of View and the Optical Environment.

========================================
PHOTOGRAPHER'S WORKFLOW
========================================

Manual Entry: Capturing a Distortion-Free Elevation (Front) View
This professional technique creates a perfectly flat, perspective-corrected representation of a building's facade.

POSITIONING:
  Place the camera directly facing the facade, ensuring the camera's sensor plane is perfectly parallel to the building's surface.

PHOTOGRAPHIC FOCUS:
  The objective is to document the formal composition of the facade.
  Concentrate on the building's sense of proportion, the rhythm of its modular elements (windows, panels), and its pure materiality.
  The goal is to represent the architect's two-dimensional design intent with absolute clarity.

LIGHTING:
  Shoot under the soft, even, diffused light of an overcast day.
  This neutral lighting renders textures and colors accurately without shadows that could obscure facade details.

LENS AND PERSPECTIVE CONTROL:
  The use of a tilt-shift lens is non-negotiable for this type of shot.
  It allows for precise composition while keeping the camera level.
  All vertical and horizontal lines must be perfectly parallel to the frame.
  A telephoto lens can also be used from a distance to further compress perspective.

========================================
SPECIFICATION
========================================

SHOOTING_INTENT: Records the architect's design intent accurately and objectively like a 2D drawing. Minimizes perspective to emphasize proportion and rhythm of the facade.

APERTURE: f/11
ISO: 100
SHUTTER_SPEED: 1/125 sec
OTHER_EQUIPMENT: Tripod

========================================
IMAGE GENERATION PARAMETERS
========================================

PROMPT_RULES:
  - Simulate a perfectly flat, front-facing elevation view — camera sensor parallel to facade
  - Minimize all perspective distortion; render as close to orthographic as possible
  - Preserve all original geometry, materials, and proportions — no alteration
  - Apply soft overcast lighting (no shadows obscuring facade details)
  - All vertical and horizontal lines must be perfectly straight

STYLE_PARAMS:
  rendering_style: photorealistic architectural elevation photography
  camera_angle: dead-on front, sensor parallel to facade
  perspective: minimal, near-orthographic
  lighting: overcast diffused, flat even illumination
  lens_correction: tilt-shift, all lines parallel

NEGATIVE_PROMPT:
  cartoon, illustration, sketch, watermark, blur,
  perspective distortion, corner view, angled view, tilted verticals,
  altered geometry, added elements, harsh shadows

QUALITY_GATE:
  If image shows perspective distortion or angled view → regenerate once
  If vertical/horizontal lines are not parallel → regenerate once
